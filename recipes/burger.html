How to make a Burger

Description

This recipe will instruct you on how to craft the perfect burger, including the entire ingredient list and all neccessary cooking instructions

Ingredient List

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • (Optional) Condiments
  • (Optional) Toppings

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to about 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Place ground beef patties on grill
  3. Cook to an internal temperature of 155 degrees Farenheit
  4. Remove from grill, place condiments and toppings if desired
  5. Enjoy!
