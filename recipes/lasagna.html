How to make Lasagna

Description

This recipe will instruct you on how to craft the perfect lasagna, including the entire ingredient list and all neccessary cooking instructions

Ingredient List

  • Ground Beef
  • Ravioli
  • Spaghetti Sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Cook ravioli for 10 minutes
  3. Pour cooked ravioli in dish, layering ground beef
  4. Pour spaghetti sauce on top
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
