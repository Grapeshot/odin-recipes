How to make Spaghetti

Description

This recipe will instruct you on how to craft the perfect bowl of spaghetti, including the entire ingredient list and all neccessary cooking instructions

Ingredient List

  • Ground Beef
  • Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil, and pour in noodles. Cook for 10 minutes
  2. Cook ground beef til browned, approximately 10 minutes
  3. Mix cooked noodles, cooked ground beef in pot with spaghetti sauce
  4. Enjoy!
