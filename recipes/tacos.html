How to make Tacos

Description

This recipe will instruct you on how to craft the perfect plate of tacos, including the entire ingredient list and all neccessary cooking instructions

Ingredient List

  • Ground Beef
  • Tacos
  • (Optional) Toppings

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef in pan until browned, about 10 minutes
  2. Heat tacos up to warm temperature if preferred
  3. Enjoy!
